SOP for Creating Request

After logging in with your credentials, you will arrive at your dashboard as shown below. Click on the Work Icon.

After selecting the work tab, click on Work Request.

Click on the + New icon found to the left of the search window.

Fill out the required fields highlighted in blue:

  • Asset ID: Identifies the specific piece of equipment needing service.
  • Description: Describe the issue or request in detail, including the specific area or workstation.
  • Priority: Choose when the request needs to be completed (Priority 1-6).
  • Requested Date and Completion Date: Fill out the expected dates and click Save.
